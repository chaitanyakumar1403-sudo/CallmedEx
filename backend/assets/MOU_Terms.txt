MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING (MOU)

Between CallMeDex (hereinafter "Platform") and the Registering Organization (hereinafter "Partner").

1. PURPOSE
This MOU outlines the terms under which the Partner agrees to list their healthcare facility, services, and available staff on the CallMeDex platform.

2. RESPONSIBILITIES OF PARTNER
- The Partner guarantees that all medical licenses, establishment certificates, and staff qualifications provided to the Platform are genuine and legally valid.
- The Partner will honor all appointments booked through the Platform.
- The Partner agrees to abide by the ABDM data sharing and privacy regulations.

3. RESPONSIBILITIES OF PLATFORM
- The Platform agrees to route patient bookings securely.
- The Platform handles initial AI document verification but holds no liability for falsified records.

4. ACCEPTANCE
By clicking the "Accept MOU & Create Account" button in your email, the Partner legally binds themselves to these terms and conditions.
